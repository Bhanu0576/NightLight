Best Night Lamp - About
A Gaming app that is incredible. Best Night Lamp is an application that
utilizes your smartphone's screen to illuminate your room utilizing any of 3
distinctive light modes -
Night Light
Mood Light
Gradient Light Lamp
Night Light
The night light in the app offers a gentle and soft light. It is bright enough to
see by; however not so bright that it dazzles you or your child.
Features:
You can use it to read before going to bed
To do your work without disturbing anyone
Enhance the skills of your child
And once the selected time is completed, its screen simply fades out and exits
the app automatically.
Mood Light
The mood light shows the rainbow colours, either in order or randomly.
Moreover, it can add a vibrant and soothing ambience to a room.
Gradient Light Lamp
Choosing the gradient Light lamp will show you different colour schemes on
your phone. Your smartphone's screen will automatically whirl as well as
swirl.
